Vision & Invitation — February 21
This year, we're building something together.
We're building a culture, not a crowd. A culture of manhood anchored in biblical values with room for expressions as dynamic as God's creation — we don't always have to meet in the same place or do the same thing, but we need to be moving together.
That starts with hearing from you. What do you need? What are you already doing that we can celebrate and partner with? What's been on your heart that you haven't felt empowered to step into? We need to see where we are so we can walk forward together — knowing each step brings clarity, that the path won't be perfectly straight, but that it gives us confidence and momentum to keep going.
It's never been about the Saturday meetings. Saturdays are for alignment, tuning, and encouragement — laying a consistent foundation. The vision has always been for more to happen outside these gatherings than inside. That can't rely on one person or a staff. It takes us as a community, bringing this to life together.
What's on your heart?
Let's build a vision for 2026 and beyond.
