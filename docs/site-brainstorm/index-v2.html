What We Mean by Culture

How well we experience and express our values—everywhere we go

At Home

The values of manhood start in our homes—how we lead our families, love our wives, and raise our children with intention.

At Journey

When you're inside our church community, you experience the values in how men lead, serve, connect, and relate to one another.

In the World

When people interact with a JLC man at work, in the neighborhood, anywhere—they experience men who live out what they believe.
