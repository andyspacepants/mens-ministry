What We're Building

A culture of manhood at Journey Life Church

Culture is how well we experience and express our values within our community.

We want to live the values of manhood so wholeheartedly that when people interact with us—at home, at Journey, at work, in our community—they experience them.

Men's ministry is where we declare, celebrate, and sharpen what it means to be a man, together.
