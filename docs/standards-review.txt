Another key element of leadership to consider is that it does not require authority. To be effective in authority, you must be a good leader, but you do not need authority to be a great leader. It is likely that you being a good leader will lead to a level of authority, but it is not required. 

To summarize - mature leadership understands the practical skills of developing people in the midst of organizational goals (whether it be the family unit, or community, or work).
